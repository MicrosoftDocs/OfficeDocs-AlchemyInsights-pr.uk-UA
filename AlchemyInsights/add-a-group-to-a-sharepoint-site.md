Для класичних сайтів рекомендовано використовувати групи зручно призначити же рівень дозволів на багато людей відразу, а не призначення дозволів одна людина за один раз.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="101df-p101">Some SharePoint team sites (including those created in Microsoft Teams) are managed by Office 365 group owners. A SharePoint group is a collection of users who all have the same set of permissions to sites and content. For classic sites, we recommend that you use groups to conveniently assign the same permission level to many people at once rather than assigning permissions one person at a time.</span></span>
